Nitro Finish

The goal was to produce a bright glossy finish, which would still show off the personality of the the wood grain. For me, the obvious choice of colour was a bright butterscotch yellow.
The premise of building this guitar was to create something that would show the signs of wear. Hence, I chose to use the much thinner vintage style nitrocelulose lacquer. There is a fair amount of controversy about the effect of the finish on the sound. The science points to this making a negligiable contribution.
